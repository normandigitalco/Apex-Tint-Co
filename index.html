Our Story

We started because we couldn't find anyone we trusted to do it right.

Apex Tint Co. opened in Allen, TX in 2019 out of pure frustration. After watching too many great cars leave shops with sloppy tint edges, bubbling film, and coating jobs that failed inside a year — we decided to do it ourselves.

Our founder came up in the industry working for a luxury dealership group in Dallas, where the standards were different. Every vehicle had to be right before it left the lot. That's the culture we brought to Apex — and it's not changed since day one.

Today we work on everything from daily commuters to six-figure exotics. The process is the same regardless of what's in the bay. No shortcuts, no excuses.
